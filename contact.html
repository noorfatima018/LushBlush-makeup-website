Shipping Policy

We currently deliver across Pakistan. Orders placed before 2 PM are processed the same day. Shipping is free for orders above PKR 2,000. Delivery options:

  • Standard: 3–5 business days
  • Express: 1–2 business days
  • Pickup: Available at select stores

Payment Methods

  • Cash on Delivery (COD)
  • JazzCash / EasyPaisa
  • Debit & Credit Cards (Visa, MasterCard)
  • Bank Transfer (details available on checkout)

Return & Refund

At Lush Blush, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. We accept returns within 30 days of receiving your order, provided the product is unused, unopened, and in its original packaging. Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility unless the item is defective or incorrect. Once we receive the returned product and confirm it meets our return criteria, we will process a refund to the original payment method within 7-10 business days, excluding shipping fees. Please note that items such as opened cosmetics are not eligible for return due to health and safety regulations.
